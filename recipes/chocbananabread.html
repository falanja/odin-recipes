Chocolate Banana Bread

Description

A classic banana bread with a chocolatey surprise!

A chocolately twist to a classic favourite

Ingredients

  • Banana xxxg
  • Caster sugar xxxg
  • Plain flour xxxg
  • Eggs lightly whisked x
  • Butter xxg
  • Baking powder xxg
  • Dark chocolate chopped xxg

Steps

  1. Squash banana
  2. Add Sugar and flour
  3. Add eggs
  4. Add butter and mix
  5. Add baking powder
  6. Add chopped chocolate and mix
  7. Line baking tray with baking paper
  8. Pour cake mix into lined baking tray
  9. Bake at 160C for 60 minutes
  10. Let cool
  11. Enjoy!

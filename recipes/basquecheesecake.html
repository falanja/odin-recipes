Basque Cheesecake

Description

A light cheesecake with a rich gooey interior and caramelized top

Simple to make and sure to be a crowd favourite

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese xxxg
  • Caster sugar xxxg
  • Eggs lightly whisked x
  • Cream xxxml
  • Vanilla essence 2 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Combine cream cheese and sugar
  2. Add eggs and mix
  3. Add cream and mix
  4. Add vanilla essence and mix
  5. Line baking tray with baking paper
  6. Pour cake mix into lined baking tray
  7. Bake at 230C for 25 minutes
  8. Let cool
  9. Enjoy!

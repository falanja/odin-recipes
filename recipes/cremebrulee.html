Creme Brulee

Description

A classic Crème Brûlée with a silky, smooth and rich custard!

Simple to make with only 4 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Cream xxxml
  • Egg yolks 4
  • Caster sugar xxxg
  • Vanilla essence xxxml

Steps

  1. Mix egg yolks with sugar
  2. Add cream
  3. Strain mix to remove clumps
  4. Add vanilla essence and mix lightly
  5. Pour into ramikins
  6. Bake in a steam bath at 160C for 25 mins
  7. Remove from oven and let cool
  8. Enjoy!
